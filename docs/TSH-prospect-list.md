## Why This Vertical

**Independent healthcare practices** are the single highest-conversion opportunity for TSH right now. Here's the math:

**Why they buy:**
- Owner-operators are the decision maker — no procurement committee, no 6-month buying cycle
- They are being eaten alive by DSOs (Dental Service Organizations) and national chains with polished websites and Google Ads budgets
- Most independent practices have outdated, slow, template-based websites built 5-10 years ago
- Local SEO is a direct revenue lever — a practice ranking #1 for "dentist near me" in their area books 3-5 extra patients per week at $200-$1,000 per visit
- They have budget: a healthy dental practice generates $600k-$2M+ annually
- HIPAA compliance is a blocker for generic web agencies — TSH's healthcare experience removes this objection instantly

**Why they convert fast:**
- The pain is visible (search their name, see the sad website yourself before calling)
- The ROI is immediate and quantifiable
- Starting price of $500 for a web package removes price objection as a gating factor
- Local Austin presence for TSH = trust advantage in Texas market

**Conversion benchmarks from market research:**
- Dental practices that modernize their website see **243% average traffic increase** and **230%+ revenue lift** within 12 months
- Austin dental SEO market: $1,200-$4,500/month → TSH's $1,000/month Essentials plan is competitive

> "I searched '[dentist near your area]' on Google — you weren't on the first page. I've helped 3 other local health practices fix this in 2 weeks, starting at $500. Worth a quick call?"

> "Hi, this is [Name] from That Software House in Austin — we build websites for healthcare practices. Before I say anything else, I just pulled up your current site and I have two specific things I noticed that are likely costing you new patients. Is now a bad time?"

**Pain points to lead with:**
1. DSOs stealing your patients with better Google rankings
2. Template website not showing up in Google Maps / local search
3. No online booking = losing patients who don't want to call
4. Website not mobile-friendly (50% of patients search on mobile)
5. No patient portal or online forms (wastes front desk time)

**TSH differentiators to mention:**
- Based in Austin (local relationship, Texas market knowledge)
- HIPAA-aware development (most web agencies can't say this)
- 2-week delivery on simple sites
- Starting as low as $1000
- Transparent pricing, no surprises

### Week 1-2: Hot Tier (Practices with verifiable outdated websites)
Target #9, #10, #11, #12, #22, #53 — businesses that have been around 10-30+ years. These almost certainly have outdated or no digital presence. Research their Google Maps listing before calling.

Target #1-#8, #16-#21, #31-#40. These have websites but rank poorly for local keywords. Use a quick SEMrush / Ahrefs free check to pull their ranking before calling — instant credibility.

### Week 5-8: Expansion
Medical spas (#56-#65), chiropractic (#66-#75), and PT (#76-#85). Slightly longer sales cycle but higher average contract value for custom portals + booking systems.

## Data Enrichment Next Steps

To fully activate this list, enrich each prospect with:
1. **Practice owner name** (Google the practice + "Dr." or check their About page)
2. **Direct email** (Format: firstname@practicename.com or info@practicename.com)
3. **Phone number** (All should be on their Google Maps listing)
4. **Current website score** (Run through Google PageSpeed Insights or GTMetrix)
5. **Google Maps ranking** (Search "[specialty] [neighborhood]" and see if they appear in top 3)
6. **Review count & rating** (High reviews + bad website = perfect prospect)

Tools recommended: Hunter.io (email finder), Semrush Free Trial (SEO audit), Google Maps (ranking check), Apollo.io (contact enrichment).
