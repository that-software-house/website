# That Software House (TSH): Company & Clientele Description

## 1. Executive Summary
That Software House (TSH) is a senior engineering studio based in Austin and San Francisco. We treat business technology as critical infrastructure, bridging the gap between high-level enterprise AI and the practical needs of local businesses. Our philosophy is "Old School": we prioritize perfecting technology, gathering precision data, and optimizing for conversions over vanity metrics like social media views.

## 2. Core Service Offerings

### I. Main Street Modernization (The $749 Migration)
We migrate established local businesses off "digital fossils"—legacy WordPress setups that are slow, insecure, and fragile—and into modern, high-performance stacks (Framer, Webflow, or Custom React).
*   **The Problem:** Constant plugin updates, security risks, and slow load times that bleed customers.
*   **The TSH Solution:** A "Clean Stack" migration that eliminates plugin dependency and provides native speed and security.
*   **Pricing:** Starts at **$749** for the launch, with a **$89/mo** maintenance and technical support retainer.

### II. AI Integration & Operational Intelligence
We turn messy, text-based archives into a searchable "AI Brain" for the business.
*   **AI Virtual Receptionist:** A 24/7 chat-based assistant that handles initial client intake, answers complex FAQs using the firm's private data, and prepares meeting summaries for the owner.
*   **Knowledge RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation):** We migrate PDF archives, client histories, and internal documents into RAG models, allowing for instant information lookup.
*   **Impact:** Drastically reduced onboarding time and automated research prep for professional service providers.

### III. High-Velocity Startup Engineering
We partner with solo founders or recently funded startups (Seed/Series A) that need to ship production-grade features immediately.
*   **The Problem:** The "Hiring Gap"—needing senior talent now but facing a 6-month recruitment cycle.
*   **The TSH Solution:** We drop in as an elastic product squad to build MVPs in weeks or harden prototypes for investor readiness and scale.

### IV. Selective "Conversion-First" Marketing
A rare, engineering-led growth offering for select SMBs willing to commit to a **12-month technical roadmap**.
*   **The Philosophy:** We do not chase "views" on TikTok or Instagram. We are **Conversion-First**.
*   **The TSH Roadmap:**
    *   **Phase 1 (The Leak Audit):** Months 1-3 focused on gathering precision data to find where leads are dropping.
    *   **Phase 2 (The Optimization):** Fixing system friction (broken flows/forms) and refining the tech stack to turn traffic into revenue.
