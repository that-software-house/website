## 4. Who Is the Perfect Target Audience for TSH?

### Primary Segments

**1. Healthcare Practices & Healthtech Companies**
Our sweet spot. We've built HIPAA-compliant AI copilots, patient data systems, and healthcare mobile apps. Dental clinics, medical spas, urgent care centers, physical therapy practices, and digital health startups all benefit from our combination of clinical-grade compliance + modern product execution.

*Why they convert*: Healthcare is underserved by tech; practitioners have budget; compliance requirements filter out cheap vendors; TSH's proven healthcare portfolio builds trust fast.

**2. Fintech Startups & Financial Services Firms**
We've scaled fintech mobile apps to 500k+ MAU, built fraud detection systems, payment integrations (Stripe, Plaid, Square), and algorithmic tools. Early-to-mid-stage fintech startups need fast execution with regulatory awareness — that's us.

*Why they convert*: Fintech founders know bad engineering is existential. They pay for quality. TSH's track record and speed close deals.

**3. Small & Mid-Size Businesses (SMBs) Needing Digital Presence**
Local businesses — dental offices, salons, restaurants, auto shops, law firms — that are operating without a modern website or with a template site that doesn't convert. They need:
- Professional web presence
- Local SEO to show up in Google
- Fast delivery at a real-world budget

*Why they convert*: High volume segment; low sales cycle; starting at $500 makes the conversation easy; TSH over-delivers vs. expectations.

**4. SaaS Startups (Seed to Series B)**
Technical founders who need to move faster than their internal team allows. They have product vision but need surge capacity — either full-squad augmentation or a specific engineering function (frontend, AI, DevOps).

*Why they convert*: Staff augmentation and MVP delivery are high-LTV services; TSH's startup credibility and speed resonate with founders.

**5. Enterprise Teams Needing Augmentation**
Mid-to-large companies (Oracle, Microsoft partners, etc.) that need to plug in senior engineers fast, without the overhead of a full-agency relationship. Our clients have included companies backed by major enterprise players.

*Why they convert*: Enterprise procurement trusts smaller, specialized shops for niche deliveries; TSH's compliance posture and senior team profile satisfy vendor vetting.
